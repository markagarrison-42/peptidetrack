Getting started
1
Open the app
Go to protocol.mg42apps.com in your browser. Works on iPhone and Android.
2
Create your account
Tap Register, enter your name, choose a username and password. No invite code needed.
3
Complete your profile
A quick 4-step setup will ask for your name, date of birth, height, starting weight, and goals.
4
Set up your protocol
Tap Protocol, create your protocol, and add your compounds with dose and frequency. The app calculates your syringe units automatically.
5
Log your doses daily
The Today tab shows everything due. Tap the + circle next to each compound to log it as taken.
What's in the app
📅
Today
Your daily dose checklist. See what's due, confirm each dose, and track your completion rate.
📋
Protocol
Manage your compounds, doses, and frequency. Built-in syringe guide shows exactly how many units to draw.
📈
Progress
Log weight and body measurements, upload progress photos (front, side, back), and compare before/after side by side.
🧬
Learn
110-peptide library with AI-generated reference cards, live research feed, and reconstitution video guides.
🧮
Calc
Reconstitution calculator. Enter your vial size and bac water amount to get the exact units to draw for any dose.
Add to your home screen

PeptideTrack works like a native app when added to your home screen. No App Store download needed.

🍎 iPhone (Safari)
  1. Open protocol.mg42apps.com in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow) at the bottom
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  4. Tap Add in the top right
  5. Open from your home screen like any app
🤖 Android (Chrome)
  1. Open protocol.mg42apps.com in Chrome
  2. Tap the three dots menu (⋮) top right
  3. Tap Add to Home screen
  4. Tap Add to confirm
  5. Open from your home screen like any app
Tips
💡
Allow notifications
When prompted, allow notifications so the app can remind you when it's time to take your dose.
💡
Use the Calc tab first
Before adding a compound to your protocol, use the Calc tab to verify your syringe units based on your vial reconstitution.
💡
Log doses in real time
Tap to confirm each dose right after you take it. Your completion rate resets each day.
